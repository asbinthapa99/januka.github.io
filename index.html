About Us

We Produce Organic Food For Your Family

Welcome to Januka Agriculture and Animal Farm, a place where nature and nurture come together. Our farm is a celebration of organic vegetables and fruits, including oranges, jackfruit, guava, and mangoes, all grown with care on our own land. Beyond our produce, we prioritize the well-being of our animals, providing them with a loving and sustainable environment. At Januka Farm, we invite you to experience the freshness of farm-to-table living and embrace a holistic approach to sustainable agriculture.

100% Organic

At 100% Organic, we produce all items organically. Our commitment to sustainability means every product is crafted using organic farming practices, ensuring a healthier choice for you and the planet.

Award Winning

Number one farm in the municipality! Explore our top-notch, sustainable produce, backed by prestigious awards and certificates. Choose freshness, choose excellence with us.
